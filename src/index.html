Raccoon City

Raccoon City — Overview

Status: City File — Restricted

Raccoon City is a midwestern city with a population of ~100,000. It is home to several Umbrella facilities, including pharmaceutical research labs and secret B.O.W testing sites.

Incident Log: Recent reports indicate unusual viral outbreaks. Local authorities are under investigation. Citizens are advised to avoid crowded areas and follow all safety instructions from law enforcement.

Alert Level: HIGH. Movement outside safe zones is strongly discouraged.

Survival Tips

Survival Tips — Emergency Protocol

Status: Accessible to Field Operatives

•Always keep weapons loaded and nearby. Firearms and melee tools are essential.

•Conserve resources: Ammo, medkits, and food are limited. Use only when necessary.

•Stay alert: Zombies and other infected entities can appear unexpectedly. Listen for sounds, avoid dark areas without backup.

•Plan your escape: Know exit points, safe houses, and routes before engaging infected zones.

•Avoid infection: Even minor bites can be fatal. Apply medical kits immediately and seek quarantine if symptoms appear.

•Communicate: Maintain radio contact with HQ. Never venture alone unless mission critical.

Warning: Failure to comply with survival protocols may result in severe injury or death. This is a live containment scenario.
